Weather Types & Explanations 🌍

☀️ Sunny

Clear sky with lots of sunlight. Often means dry, warm weather and bright days.

🌧️ Rainy

Clouds produce rain, sometimes with drizzle or heavy showers. Brings fresh smell and cooler air.

❄️ Snowy

Snowflakes falling from the sky, covering the ground in white. Usually happens in cold regions or winter.

⛈️ Thunderstorm

Storms with lightning and thunder. Often comes with heavy rain, gusty winds, and dramatic sky.

🌫️ Fog

Thick mist near the ground, making visibility low. Happens often in mornings or coastal areas.

☁️ Cloudy

Sky is full of clouds, blocking some sunlight. Often feels cooler and can lead to rain.

🌬️ Windy

Fast-moving air. Can feel refreshing or strong, and can carry dust, pollen, or sea breeze.
